Chocolate Chip Cookies

Easy to make and great with milk!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups brown sugar.
  • 1 cup butter, softened.
  • 2 large eggs.
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 1 ½ cups semi-sweet chocolate chips.

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time. Gradually stir in flour until well combined; fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 2 inches apart. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Savor and enjoy with a glass of milk.
